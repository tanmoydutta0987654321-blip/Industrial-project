National Digital Identity System

A National Digital Identity System provides every citizen with a secure and unique digital identity. It enables seamless access to government and private services while ensuring privacy, security, and transparency.

Mission

To provide a secure, reliable, and accessible digital identity platform for all citizens.

Vision

To build a digitally empowered nation where identity verification is fast, safe, and universally accessible.
