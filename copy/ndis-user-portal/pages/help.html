Frequently Asked Questions

How to create a Digital ID?

To create a Digital ID, visit the Home page and click on 'Register'. You will need to provide your full name, mobile number, and upload valid identity documents.

How to update my profile?

You can update your profile by navigating to the Dashboard, clicking on the 'Edit Profile' button in the Profile Card.

How to check verification status?

Use the 'Check Verification Status' tool on the Dashboard or Services page.
